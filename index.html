Develop open-source tools for research and teaching, including quicklook.

Enjoy basketball, cooking, camping, and woodworking.

Delivered invited talks for academic and public audiences, and founded an astronomy community initiative to promote public engagement in astronomy.

Contribute data-driven explainers with BetterGov, including this analysis of the Philippine Congress.
